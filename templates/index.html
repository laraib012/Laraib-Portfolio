Project 1

CESD Society Website – Namal University

Project 2

Ground Water Contamination Model

Developed a numerical simulation model to study groundwater contamination spread using Python. The model utilizes Gauss-Seidel and Jacobi iterative methods to solve for hydraulic head distribution and contaminant concentration in a defined domain.
